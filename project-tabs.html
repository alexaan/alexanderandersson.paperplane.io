Master Project: The Molecular Modeling Language GEMAL

Main technologies used: Troux Architect (Metis), C#

{{selectedData.desc}}

Traditional modelling languages focus on modeling according to a specific modeling perspective, e.g. being primarily process-oriented or object-oriented. Even if newer languages such as UML and EEML combines several perspectives, they typically do so by combining several single-perspective modelling languages. Because of this is the resulting languages are large and cumbersome to learn and to use.

An alternative approach is so-called holistic modelling languages, where a limited number of concepts can be combined in an orthogonal fashion. The task set out to work further on a proposal for such a language (GEMAL), implement it in the Troux family of meta-modelling tools (previously 'Metis'), and evaluate it through case studies and empirical evaluation techniques.
